Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking?

To make a booking, simply browse our rooms, select your dates, choose your room, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to be logged in to complete your booking. Once confirmed, you'll receive an email with your booking details.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation is available up to 7 days before check-in. Cancellations within 7 days of check-in will incur a 50% charge of the total booking amount. No-shows will be charged the full amount.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and M-PESA. All payments are processed securely. For M-PESA payments, you'll receive a prompt on your phone to complete the transaction.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 48 hours before check-in. Simply go to "My Trips", select the booking you want to modify, and click "Modify". Note that changes may affect the price.

What activities are included?

Our sanctuary offers game drives, guided bird walks, horse riding, boat safaris, and cultural experiences. Some activities may require an additional fee. Check our activities page for more details and pricing.

Is breakfast included?

Breakfast is included with all room bookings. We serve a full English/Kenyan breakfast from 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM. Special dietary requirements can be accommodated with advance notice.

What should I bring?

We recommend bringing comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, a hat, camera, binoculars for wildlife viewing, and light clothing for the day with a warm jacket for evenings. Don't forget your swimming gear!

Is there Wi-Fi available?

Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available in all rooms and common areas. However, due to our remote location, the connection may vary. We encourage you to disconnect and enjoy nature!
Booking Help

How do I find my booking?

Log in to your account and go to "My Trips". You'll see all your upcoming and past bookings. You can also find your booking confirmation in your email inbox.

Can I book for someone else?

Yes, you can make a booking for someone else. Just enter their details in the guest information section during checkout. They'll receive the confirmation email.

What is check-in/out time?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM. Check-out is at 10:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional fees.
Payment Help

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are processed automatically when you cancel within the free cancellation period. Refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear in your account, depending on your bank.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, all payments are processed through secure, encrypted connections. We never store your full credit card details. For M-PESA, all transactions are protected by Safaricom's security.

Do you offer discounts?

We offer special rates for longer stays, group bookings, and return guests. Check our promotions page or subscribe to our newsletter for exclusive offers.
