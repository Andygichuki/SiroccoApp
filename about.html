Our Story

Founded in 2010, Siroco Wildlife Sanctuary began as a dream to create a haven where wildlife and humans could coexist in harmony. Nestled on the shores of Lake Naivasha, our sanctuary spans over 500 acres of pristine savanna, acacia woodland, and lakefront property.

What started as a small conservation project has grown into one of Kenya's most beloved eco-lodges, welcoming thousands of visitors each year while maintaining our commitment to conservation and community development.
